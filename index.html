    NIST Computer Security Resource Center: NIST PUB list (updated 12/2021)

    Nearly all commercial and residential consumers are concerned about modern cybersecurity practices. Something as simple as NOT reusing passwords can add a tremendous layer of protection for most users. Many anti-virus software companies include a password creater; Norton 360 for example. Google Chrome also includes this feature. Users have the ability to save login and password information.

    Another option is to use or write your own password creator. In this method, once the password is set, copy the password to a document along with the details of the site or software.

    As an additional layer of protection, add 2-step verification. Google offers this service for account holders for free. Even if your email account has been "hacked ", the intruder would also have to "hack " your 2-step verification source; which could be your phone or a token. In most cases, that is just too much work for the average hacker. They would prefer to go after softer targets.
